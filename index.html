Uyqudan uyg'onganda aytiladigan duo

لْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ

Tarjima:Praise is to Allah Who gives us life after He has caused us to die and to Him is the return.” Alhamdu lillaahil-lathee 'ahyaanaa ba'da maa 'amaatanaa wa'ilayhin-nushoor.
